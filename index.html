Frequently asked questions

What is Blockade?
Blockade is a two-player abstract strategy game on a 7x7 board. Each turn you move your pawn and place a block. The player who cannot move loses.

Do I need an internet connection?
No. Blockade works fully offline.

Game modes
You can play against the AI or with another person on the same device (pass-and-play).

Languages
The app supports multiple interface languages. Change language in Settings inside the app.

Privacy

Blockade does not require an account. Gameplay is local to your device.

System requirements

  • iOS 15.6 or later
  • iPhone and iPad
