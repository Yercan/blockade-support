Privacy Policy

Blockade · 7x7 abstract strategy for iOS

Last updated: April 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Blockade ("the App") handles information when you use the iOS application developed by Yasin Ercan Ozdemir ("we", "us"). By using the App, you agree to this policy.

Summary

Blockade is designed to be played offline. The App does not require you to create an account. We do not sell your personal data. We do not integrate third-party advertising or analytics SDKs in the App.

Information the App processes

Gameplay and app settings. Gameplay occurs on your device. Your preferences (e.g. language, sound settings) may be stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage (such as UserDefaults). This data stays on your device unless you delete the App or erase it through your device settings.

No online multiplayer. The App does not transmit your gameplay to our servers for multiplayer, because online multiplayer is not implemented.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not ask for your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.
  • We do not use the App to track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is necessary to operate the App on the device.

Apple and App Store

When you download or update the App from the Apple App Store, Apple may process information according to its own policies. Apple may provide developers with aggregated or diagnostic information related to app performance and crashes. For details, see Apple's privacy policy and your device settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the developer via GitHub: github.com/Yercan
